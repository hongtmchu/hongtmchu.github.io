Research Interests

Computational approaches to optimal transport and Wasserstein distributionally robust optimization. I specialize in designing fast computational methods and efficient algorithms for robust machine learning and deep neural networks.

Short Bio

Hong T.M. Chu is an Assistant Professor at VinUniversity, Vietnam. Previously, she was a Research Fellow at the National University of Singapore, where she earned her PhD under Professor Kim-Chuan Toh.
