Research interests

Optimization methods in computational optimal transport and machine learning.

My research focuses on the computational approaches to optimal transport and Wasserstein distributionally robust optimization problems. Specifically, I am interested in developing advanced optimization algorithms to efficiently solve various variants of optimal transport problems, along with the tractable interpretation of the minimax problems that arise from Wasserstein robust optimization.
